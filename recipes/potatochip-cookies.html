Potato Chip Cookies

Description

These are sweet and salty potato chip cookies from 1976. Recipe from @bdylanhollis on TikTok.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 & 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 8 oz bag chips
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Cream butter & sugar until fuffy.
  2. Beat in eggs and add Vanilla extract.
  3. Fold in flour + baking powder on low until just combined.
  4. Add in 1/2 of the crushed chips & chocolate.
  5. Chill, then roll 2in balls & roll in chips.
